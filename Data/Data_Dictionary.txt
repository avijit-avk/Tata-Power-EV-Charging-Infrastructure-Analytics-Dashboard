NOTE ON FIXES APPLIED
  roi_bengaluru_districts was corrected before this dictionary was produced:
  (1) 23 projects with negative 5yr ROI were reclassified from Priority_Tier
      'Medium' to 'Low'. A negative ROI project cannot be medium priority.
  (2) A new column 'Capitalized_Land_Lease_10yr_Lakh' was added to make the
      Total_CAPEX formula fully transparent. Total_CAPEX includes 10 years of
      land lease capitalised per Ind AS 116 lease accounting standards.
  All other four datasets were clean and required no changes.

TABLE 2: charging_sessions.csv
================================================================================
Description : Transactional log of 114,000 individual EV charging sessions
              across all 65 stations in Bengaluru. Records who charged, where,
              when, how much energy was consumed, and the cost incurred.
Rows        : 114,000
Columns     : 7
Primary Key : session_id
Foreign Keys: customer_id  -> customers.csv
              station_id   -> charging_stations.csv
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

COLUMN              DATA TYPE   RANGE / VALUES            DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
session_id          Text        S00001 to S114000         Unique identifier for each
                                                          charging session. Format:
                                                          S followed by 5-digit number.

customer_id         Text        C0001 to C8000            Identifier of the customer who
                                                          initiated the session. Links to
                                                          customers.csv for demographic
                                                          and vehicle information.

station_id          Integer     2 to 197                  Identifier of the charging
                                                          station used. Links to
                                                          charging_stations.csv. Note:
                                                          IDs are not sequential; gaps
                                                          exist in the numbering.

session_start_time  Text        2024-08-01 to             Timestamp when the charging
                   (datetime)   2024-12-31                session began. Format:
                                                          YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS. Covers
                                                          approximately 5 months of
                                                          operational data.

kwh_charged         Decimal     1.74 to 84.63             Total energy delivered to the
                                                          vehicle during the session in
                                                          kilowatt-hours. Varies by
                                                          battery size, state of charge
                                                          at arrival, and session
                                                          duration.

cost_per_kwh        Decimal     13.5 / 18.0 / 22.5        Per-unit tariff applied to the
                                                          session in Rupees per kWh.
                                                          Matches the three tariff tiers
                                                          in the ROI dataset. Determined
                                                          by the station's charger type.

total_cost          Decimal     23.51 to 1,747.41         Total amount charged to the
                                                          customer for the session in
                                                          Rupees. Formula: kwh_charged ×
                                                          cost_per_kwh. Verified accurate
                                                          for all 114,000 records.

================================================================================
CROSS-DATASET RELATIONSHIPS
================================================================================

  charging_sessions.customer_id  -->  customers.customer_id
      Each session is linked to one registered customer.
      114,000 sessions across 8,000 customers (avg ~14.25 sessions/customer).

  charging_sessions.station_id  -->  charging_stations.station_id
      Each session occurred at one of 65 operational stations.
      All session station_ids verified present in charging_stations.

  charging_stations.district_name  -->  districts.district_name
      Each station belongs to one of 18 districts.
      All station districts verified present in districts master table.

  roi_bengaluru_districts.District  -->  districts.district_name
      Each proposed project is in one of the 18 planning districts.
      ROI table uses 'District'; districts table uses 'district_name'.
